The Department of Electrical Engineering is one of the oldest departments of the National Institute of Technology, Warangal (NITW). Established as one of the major departments of the Institute, since its inception in 1959, the Department of Electrical Engineering has been actively engaged in teaching and research in diverse fields of Electrical Engineering. With excellent faculty, the Department of Electrical Engineering offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and graduate (M.Tech) in Power Electronics & Drives and Power Systems and research (Ph.D) programmes.
